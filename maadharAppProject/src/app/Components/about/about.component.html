Why mAadhar card

Accurate Identification

A unique identification number enables accurate identification of individuals within a system. It provides a reliable and consistent means to distinguish one person from another, ensuring that each individual's identity can be accurately verified and authenticated.

Efficient Service Delivery

With a unique identification number, government agencies and service providers can streamline their processes and deliver services more efficiently. It helps eliminate duplication, reduce errors, and improve the overall speed and accuracy of service delivery, whether it's for healthcare, education, social benefits, or other public services.

Fraud Prevention

A unique identification number acts as a deterrent against identity theft, fraud, and misuse of public resources. By linking an individual's identity to a unique number, it becomes more challenging for someone to impersonate another person or fraudulently access benefits or services meant for someone else.
